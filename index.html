RangerLog — Devon Rangers
Built for ranger teams managing green spaces across Torbay. Log wildlife, hazards, invasive species, infrastructure and more — with photo capture, GPS and OS grid references.
Build stages
Stage 1 — Photo, GPS & field logging ✅
Stage 2 — Interactive map ✅
Stage 3 — Full categories, tasks & edit/delete ✅
Stage 4 — OS Grid Reference integration ✅
Stage 5 — Google Sheets sync ✅
Stage 6 — Team messaging ✅
Stage 7 — Export & reporting ✅
Stage 8 — Paths management tab ✅
Stage 9 — Photo saving (IndexedDB) ✅
